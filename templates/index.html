📖 How to Use

🎯 Detection Steps

  1. Prepare the Engine
    • Park safely and reduce background noise
    • Keep the engine at idle
  2. Record the Sound
    • Place device near the engine (6-12 inches)
    • Tap "Record Audio" or upload audio file
    • Record for 5-30 seconds
  3. Repeat 3 Times
    • Record the engine sound three (3) times
    • Use different angles if possible
  4. Result Rule
    • If fault detected 2 out of 3 times, it's the final result
    • Example: Clicking (2/3) → Final: Clicking Fault
  5. View Result
    • Detected engine condition will be displayed
    • Re-test if results are inconsistent

✅ Detection Confidence Guide

3/3 High Confidence
2/3 Accepted Result
1/3 Uncertain (Try Again)
0/3 Normal / No Fault

🔧 Possible Causes

⚙️ Timing Belt Issue
  • Worn or loose timing belt
  • Incorrect belt tension
  • Misalignment or aging belt
🔊 Clicking Sound
  • Worn valve lifters
  • Low or dirty engine oil
  • Poor lubrication
🔨 Rod Knock
  • Worn connecting rod bearings
  • Low oil pressure
  • Severe engine wear
💥 Engine Knocking
  • Low-octane or incorrect fuel
  • Carbon buildup
  • Incorrect ignition timing
  • Engine overheating
